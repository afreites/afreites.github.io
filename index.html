Weather Dashboard

Welcome to the Weather Dashboard. Weather is defined as the state of the atmosphere with respect to heat or cold, wetness or dryness, calm or storm, clearness, or cloudiness. It includes temperature, pressure, humidity, clouds, wind, precipitation, and fog.

The Weather Dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of weather conditions such as: Cloudiness, Humidity, Temperature and Wind Speed. Click on the drop-down or on the icons to learn more about each. Visit the "comparisons" tab click or the "Data" tab for a list of cities. Enjoy your visit and remember to share.
